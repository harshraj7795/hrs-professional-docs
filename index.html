About Me

Bridging data engineering, business, and AI

I've always believed the best data work doesn't end in a dashboard — it ends in a decision someone feels confident making. That conviction has driven a career spent across the entire data stack: engineering the pipelines that move and shape data, modeling it into something trustworthy, and delivering the insight that drives action.

Over 6+ years — and a progression from Data Analyst to Senior Data Analyst at BenchSci — I've learned to be just as comfortable building the data foundation as communicating what it reveals. I write clean, well-tested dbt transformations on BigQuery, tune warehouse performance, and treat governance and reliability as first-class, then turn that foundation into reporting leaders actually use.

What excites me most today is agentic AI. I design self-serve analytics workflows that connect Claude and Cursor to data via MCP — so teams ask questions in plain language and get governed, audit-trailed answers on their own. The result is measurable: fewer reporting bottlenecks, dramatically lower costs, and insights delivered in days, not weeks.

I partner closely with GTM, Sales, and Product teams and advise senior leadership on the metrics that move the business. I hold an M.Eng from UBC and a B.Tech (Honors) in Electrical Engineering.

Agentic Just-in-Time Self-Serve Analytics

75% ↓ requests
◆ Problem

Business teams waited days — sometimes weeks — on the analytics team for every new dashboard or data question, creating a bottleneck that slowed decisions across GTM, Sales, and Product.

◆ Tools

Claude, BigQuery MCP, reusable Claude Skills, dbt-modeled data, governance & compliance guardrails.

◆ Solution

Built reusable, governed skills connecting Claude to BigQuery via MCP — guiding users from a plain-language question to a trusted insight, with compliance rules and an automatic audit trail on every answer.

◆ Impact

Teams now self-generate critical insights in a day instead of waiting on Analytics — reducing analytics inbound requests by ~75% and freeing the team for higher-value work.

02

Automated Executive Reporting Pipeline

Daily, hands-off
◆ Problem

Senior leadership needed a current, reliable view of key metrics every morning — but assembling it was a manual, repetitive process prone to delay.

◆ Tools

Cursor, BigQuery MCP, Slack MCP, dbt data models, Apps Script (web-hosted HTML dashboard).

◆ Solution

Designed an agentic workflow in Cursor that reads dbt-modeled BigQuery tables, refreshes a live dashboard each morning, drafts a customized Slack highlights message, and shares the live link — all from a finalized, iterated template.

◆ Impact

Replaced a manual reporting cycle with a fully automated daily refresh — leadership opens up-to-date metrics and curated highlights every morning, with zero analyst effort.

03

Secure Enterprise Data Exchange & Governance

Governed sharing
◆ Problem

Enterprise clients wanted visibility into their own usage analytics, while internal account teams needed strict, role-based access boundaries to the same data.

◆ Tools

dbt post-hooks, row-level security (RLS), Google Cloud Storage (GCS), BigQuery, partnership with Infrastructure.

◆ Solution

Used dbt post-hooks to enforce RLS for account-team access and to write curated outputs to a GCS bucket — then partnered with Infra to operationalize it as a secure data-exchange serving enterprise clients.

◆ Impact

Enabled governed, self-service data sharing with enterprise customers while enforcing strict internal access control — turning analytics into a client-facing value-add.
